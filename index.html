A Brief Overview

Have you ever wondered how Siri in Apple devices, Cortana in Windows devices, programs such as Chatbots and modern home appliances such as the iRobot Roomba (smart vaccum cleaner) work? If not then you probably have wondered how the computer in the game of computer chess almost always wins. All of these examples are the gifts of a technology that is making our lives much easier. Artificially Intelligent computers and programs have unknowingly become a part of our daily lives and they are having a major impact as well. They are being used in simple voice recognitition softwares to advanced robots.

        Artificial Intelligence(AI) is the branch of computer science that studies artificially intelligent computers and programs. It is also referred to computer programs with special abilities. As the name suggests, AI are computer programs that have the ability to learn on their own. They do not require programmers to program them to do things. The ability to adapt according to the environment that they are kept in, improving itself and teaching itself to do things with proper reasoning makes a computer intelligent and makes them worthy of being titled as Artificial Intelligence.
